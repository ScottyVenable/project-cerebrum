# Fathom & Project Cerebrum Visual Identity

## Brand: Fathom
**Aesthetic:** "Synthetic Organic" – The intersection of deep consciousness and high-bandwidth tech.

### Color Palette: "Synaptic Void"
*   **Deep Obsidian (`#0A0A0B`):** Base/Background. Represents the vastness of the mind.
*   **Neural Violet (`#8B5CF6`):** Primary Accent. Represents active electrical signals and neural pathways.
*   **Electric Cyan (`#22D3EE`):** Secondary Accent. Used for data visualization and UI "Active" states.
*   **Silver Silk (`#F8FAFC`):** Typography. Clean, high-readability.

## Project: Cerebrum
**Aesthetic:** "Industrial Minimalism" – Rugged, functional, and transparent.

### Design Elements
*   **Typography:** Inter (Sans-serif) for UI; JetBrains Mono for data/status readouts.
*   **Shapes:** Thin lines (0.5pt), glassmorphic blurs, and circular synaptic patterns.
*   **Iconography:** Abstract nodes connected by filaments; minimalist brain profile.

## Assets (Mockup Specifications)
*   **Logo Concept:** A stylized Greek letter 'Psi' (Ψ) where the vertical stem is a digital circuit board and the outer arms are organic filaments.
*   **UI Layout:** Dark mode by default, high-contrast, with "pulsing" indicators for signal quality (Green = 90%+, Yellow = 60-90%, Red = <60%).
