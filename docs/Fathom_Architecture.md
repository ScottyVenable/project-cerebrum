# Fathom Architecture & Ethical Framework

## Mission Statement
**Fathom** is a solo venture dedicated to the ethical convergence of neurotechnology and artificial intelligence. We believe that the next frontier of computing is internal, and it must be built with absolute privacy, affordability, and human agency at its core.

## 1. Core Principles
*   **Neural-Self Sovereignty:** Your brain data is the ultimate expression of your "self." Fathom systems are "Local-First," meaning raw EEG data is processed and stored on-device, never in the cloud.
*   **Radical Affordability:** BCI should not be a luxury for the elite. We prioritize off-the-shelf hardware (Raspberry Pi, open-source sensors) to democratize neural-access.
*   **Transparency of Intent:** Fathom BCIs must clearly distinguish between "conscious commands" and "unconscious state-changes" to ensure the user remains in control.

## 2. Business Architecture (1-Man Tech Co)
### Phase A: Foundation (Current)
*   Development of Project Cerebrum (Hardware/Software prototype).
*   Community-driven research and open-source contributions.

### Phase B: Productization
*   Creation of "Fathom Kits"—pre-configured SD cards and lightweight 3D-printable headset frames.
*   Subscription-free local AI models.

### Phase C: Scaling
*   Partnerships with psychological researchers for "BCI-assisted therapy" tools.
*   Development of low-power ASICs for integrated wearable neuro-processing.

## 3. Data Privacy Ethics
Fathom adopts the "Neuro-Rights" framework:
1.  **Mental Privacy:** Protection from unauthorized data extraction.
2.  **Identity:** Protection from system-induced changes to personal identity.
3.  **Agency:** Ensuring the human remains the primary decision-maker in any AI-assisted loop.

## 4. Future Tech-Stack Roadmap
*   **Near-Term:** Python, LSL, TFLite, Raspberry Pi 5.
*   **Mid-Term:** Rust-based signal processing (for safety/speed), custom PCB integration.
*   **Long-Term:** Integration with LLMs for "Thought-to-Query" interfaces.
